# Office Cleaning SOP

## Scope

Routine office cleaning procedures for shared desks, meeting rooms, pantry, washrooms, and common areas.

## Daily Tasks

1. Empty bins and replace liners.
2. Dust and wipe desks, counters, and touchpoints.
3. Vacuum or mop floors.
4. Clean and disinfect washrooms.
5. Refill soap, tissue, and sanitizer.

## Weekly Tasks

1. Deep-clean glass, partitions, and corners.
2. Sanitize high-contact devices and handles.
3. Inspect supplies and report low stock.

## Quality Checklist

- No visible dust on horizontal surfaces.
- Floors free of debris and stains.
- Washrooms sanitized and restocked.
- Issues logged with photo evidence if needed.
