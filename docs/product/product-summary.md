# Product Summary

## Vision

Build an operations platform that helps cleaning teams plan, execute, and verify work with clear workflows and measurable quality.

## Target Users

- Operations managers
- Site supervisors
- Field cleaners

## Core Problems

- Inconsistent task execution across locations
- Low visibility into daily completion and quality
- Slow handoff between office planning and field work

## MVP Outcomes

- Standardized task templates
- Daily schedule and assignment tracking
- Proof-of-work capture and completion status
- Basic reporting for completion and issues

## Success Metrics

- Weekly task completion rate
- Repeat issue rate per site
- Time to assign and close tasks
